Welcome!


I am Julieta Arancio. I am a researcher in Science and Technology Studies, an advocate for democratization of science and technology and a mentor for open hardware projects.

I recently got my PhD, where I studied the open science hardware movement in the Global South. You can access everything about my thesis in the PhD section of this website.

I have a background in Environmental Sciences and a love for libre/open source philosophy, which I try to mix in the projects I work on. See the Projects section for more info or take a look at my CV.

I write down ideas on topics that interest me in my blog, Hyphae.
